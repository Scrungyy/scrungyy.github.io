About Cybersecurity

This class typically covers the fundamentals of cybersecurity, such as the different types of cyber threats, the basics of network security, and the importance of keeping systems secure. Students will learn about the various types of cyber attacks, including phishing, malware, ransomware, and Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks. They will also learn about the various tools and techniques used to detect and prevent these attacks. Additionally, the class will cover topics such as authentication, encryption, and data privacy. Also, the class will discuss the importance of creating a secure and reliable network infrastructure, and how to develop an effective security policy.
